How to Play TRUNFO

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin a new game.
  2. Look at your card's attributes (Size, Speed, Lifespan, Strength).
  3. Click on an attribute you think will beat the computer's card.
  4. The player with the higher value wins both cards.
  5. In case of a draw, the cards go to a draw pile.
  6. The winner of the next round claims the draw pile.
  7. The game ends when one player collects all cards.
